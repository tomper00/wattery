Smart Nudging

Wattery nudges guests to use less water by providing real-time feedback on their water usage. This feedback can be delivered in a variety of ways, such as:

  • Displaying water usage data on in-room tablets
  • Providing guests with a daily water usage report
  • Offering rewards for reducing water usage
Real-time Monitoring

Track water usage in real-time at room and property levels.

Transparent Data Display

Provide clear and accessible data to guests and staff.

Gamification

Encourage sustainable practices through challenges and rewards.
